Thesis

Experimental Thesis in Operating Systems

Thesis title: "Steganografia su PDF" ("Steganography on PDF files")

Supervisor: Ing. Domenico Daniele Bloisi

Short description: The aim of my thesis work was to propose a new steganographic technique that would make it possible to insert and extract "secret messages" from PDF files ('Portable Document Format') files.
The "iText" library for the Java language was used to read, write and edit PDF files.

Project

"RoboCup 2022 Open Research Challenge"

[11/07/2022 – 17/07/2022]

As a member of Team UNIBAS WOLVES of the "University of Basilicata" in collaboration with Team SPQR of the University "La Sapienza" of Rome, we participated in theOpen Research Challenge of the RoboCup 2022, the World Robotics Championships, held in Bangkok (Thailandia), presenting the Project
“MARIO: Modular and Extensible Architecture for Computing Visual Statistics in RoboCup SPL”.

This is a fully automated end-to-end architecture for computing game statistics in the RoboCup SPL, based on the latest artificial intelligence and computer vision techniques, with which we won the challenge (in ex-aequo with the B-Human team).

For more details and information, please visit the website.

"SciRoc Challenge 2021"

[06/09/2021 – 10/09/2021]

European Robotics and Smart City competition held in Bologna, where I participated in a task on performing and interpreting Italian Sign Language.

The objective of the competition was to use a robot, specifically the robot TIAGo, to interact with deaf or hard-of-hearing users using Italian Sign Language (LIS).

The work was carried out in a team, consisting of students from the University of Basilicata, with whom we won the challenge.
